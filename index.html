InstaShop

Another notable project I worked on is InstaShop, shopify for Africa. The main purpose is to provide users with a quick and easy way to create their own eCommerce websites. Users can visit the site, choose a website design template, and within seconds have their own fully functional eCommerce website.

I built InstaShop using Django, PostgreSQL, and AWS. One of the unique aspects was the use of different microservices to deploy users' eCommerce stores under their own domain names. InstaShop was successfully used by 50 users, demonstrating its practicality and effectiveness in serving the eCommerce needs of users in Africa. This project highlights my ability to manage and deliver complex solutions independently, leveraging advanced technologies to meet user needs efficiently.
